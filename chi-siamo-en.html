About Us

CLAN IRON PINO was born in Bolzano as a reference point for those who want to approach kickboxing and boxing in a serious, motivating, and authentic environment. We are a group, a family, a school of discipline and respect.

Our approach combines technique, passion, and mindset. Every member is followed with attention and dedication to grow not only as an athlete but also as a person. Our strength lies in the group, in consistency, and in the energy we bring every day in and out of the ring.

Discipline

Every training session is a challenge with oneself. We learn to overcome our limits with consistency and self-control.

Respect

Respect is the foundation of our method. Respect for the coach, for the teammate, for the opponent, and for the ring.
